THOR THUNDER

Thor Odinson, sometimes referred to by his title as the God of Thunder, is a fictional character portrayed by Chris Hemsworth in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) media franchise,
based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name and the Norse mythological god of the same name.

He might seem like a lightweight compared to other superheroes, but Spider-Man has shown he can be stronger than Thor - multiple times.
When it comes to super strength, Spider-Man often ranks fairly low compared to powerhouses like Thor and Hulk.
